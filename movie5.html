MOON KNIGHT

Moon Knight is more of a character study slowly unlearning its own potential and becoming less of a superhero story. It's definitely not a faithful adaptation. I also wouldn't say it's a bad one, either. The season steered the character in a direction that still managed to pull me in despite the unfamiliarity.
